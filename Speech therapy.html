Speech Therapy

At TrueLife Home Healthcare, we believe communication is the key to connection, learning, and independence. Our home-based Speech Therapy services are designed to support children and adults facing challenges with speech, language, voice, or swallowing — all from the safety and comfort of their own home. Delivered by our highly qualified, DHA-licensed speech therapists, each session is tailored to the individual’s specific goals, age, and needs — whether developmental, neurological, or injury-related.

Adult and Pediatric

Our Adult and Pediatric care services provide comprehensive, age-appropriate medical support tailored to individual needs, ensuring compassionate, effective treatment for both adults and children across a wide range of health conditions.

Cognitive Linguistic Function

Cognitive linguistic function therapy enhances thinking, memory, problem-solving, and communication skills through personalized exercises, helping individuals improve daily functioning after neurological conditions or injuries.

Swallowing Training and Education

Swallowing training and education focus on improving safe eating and drinking through personalized exercises, techniques, and guidance for individuals with swallowing difficulties due to medical or neurological conditions.

Voice Therapy

Voice therapy helps improve vocal strength, clarity, and quality through personalized exercises and techniques, supporting recovery from voice disorders, strain, or medical conditions affecting speech.

Reading and Writing Training

Reading and writing training enhances literacy skills through structured, personalized exercises, supporting individuals with learning difficulties or those recovering from neurological conditions to regain effective communication and academic abilities.
